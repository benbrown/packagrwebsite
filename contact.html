About Packagr

Packagr is a multi-platform digital publishing tool developed by XOXCO in Austin, TX. We are a small team with over a decade of experience publishing to the web.

We are passionate about the future of digital publishing. Packagr was designed out of a growing need to reach the wide variety of device types without impeding on existing workflows and systems.
