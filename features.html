Multi-Format Publishing

Packagr was designed to improve the digital publishing workflow. Once a project has been created, it can be published to a number of formats and delivered to most types of devices. As a result, your content reaches a much wider audience.

Create a project once and simply choose your delivery method. Packagr handles all of the content formatting for each medium, so you can focus on creating a great digital publication.

New Revenue Channels

Sell digital products through app stores, online bookstores, newsletter subscriptions and more. Create new products for a variety of digital devices using your existing catalog of back content.

It's easier than ever to sell eBooks through Amazon, or offer direct downloads on your own site. Create a curated version of your blog and publish it as an iPad magazine, or send a weekly digest to your email subscriber list.

CMS Agnostic

Create new projects from scratch, or use Packagr's content importing functionality to create projects from existing web content. Packagr can import content from a variety of sources to add value to your existing publishing process.

Packagr is designed to work in conjunction with your current publishing workflow. Content can be imported from an RSS feed, via our WordPress plugin, or a number of other methods.

Cloud Hosted

Create, edit and publish projects on a single account. Your content is stored and processed in Amazon's cloud, able to be accessed from any computer.

Real-Time Previews

Use the previewer to visualize your project in any format and multiple screen-sizes during the creation process. All content and style changes are reflected in real-time, so you know exactly how your project will look once published.

Create CSS-based templates to perfectly match your project's specifications. Projects in Packagr are typographically elegant and responsive by default, designed for legibility on any screen size.
